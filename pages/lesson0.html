Lesson 0 / How to choose a laptop?

Basically we have two types of laptops: First of all, these are for business and premium class, usually they are not cheap and not powerful, but for a compact device they are very good and can work for about 8-12 hours or more, in any case, business laptops look good and minimalistic.

Best business laptops in my opinion:

• Lenova Thinkpad x1

• Dell xps 13

• Macbook air m2

• HP ENVY

• Asus Zenbook 14

• Surface Laptop 4

In the business category there are also 2 in 1 laptops, which are a laptop and a tablet at the same time.
