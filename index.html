How to use the site?

The platform has start pages such as “Home” and “Contacts” for getting to know and contacting the author. There is also a lesson section for studying and learning, it starts from lesson 0 and will be published more and more in some periods.

If you're using a laptop or something with a large screen, the list of lessons will be on the left, or if you're using a phone, you'll need to click on the “hamburger” (three lines in the upper left corner of the screen) to open the menu.
